ABOUT US

Maven Mentors is a young and aspiring startup initiated by students of IIT Ropar. It aims at providing JEE Aspirants Mentors from Top Tier Colleges in India so as to guide them in exploring different fields, learn new skills and selecting their branch in college. We think that once you are done with JEE it's your time to get out of PCM Centralized studies and explore the world around you. But sometimes it becomes difficult to start and explore things out. As we were in the same situation 2 years ago we created a special Mentorship Programme to help you learn skills like programming,finance etc. and fill your JOSAA preferences.
