ABOUT US

Maven Mentors is a young and aspiring startup initiated by students of IIT Ropar. It aims at providing JEE Aspirants best mentors so as to guide them in exploring different fields, learn new skills and selecting their major in college.

What do we do at MAVEN MENTORS?

  • We connect you to best mentors based on your interests after JEE.
  • Mentors will be students of top tier colleges in India selected after a rigorous interview procedure.
  • These Mentors will guide you in learning new skills like programming, finance etc. and exploring different fields before selecting your major in college.
  • They will help you fill your JOSAA preferences in the most efficient way to select best possible branch and college based on your interests and JEE Rank.
  • If you are inclined towards a particular skill then we will connect you to a mentor who is already performing well in it.
  • You can chat with your mentor or request a call back ( they will have to reply within 18 hours ).
  • All those conversations will be on our exclusive app so as to ensure the quality of mentorship.
  • After each week a portal will be floated on the app to give ratings to your mentor. We will keep a close look at these ratings.
  • You can reach us anytime if you face any specific issue with your mentor.
Registration Procedure
  • Click on the REGISTER NOW button, fill your basic info and any special requirements in your mentor.
  • We will contact you within 24 Hours of your registration and discuss when to start your mentorship.
  • It will be a 4-week program and its fees will be Rs 1100/-.
